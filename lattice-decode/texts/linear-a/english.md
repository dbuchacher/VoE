# Linear A — Plain English

Readings of Minoan Linear A tablets (~1800–1450 BCE).
Unknown words are marked [?]. Uncertain readings are in brackets.
Personal names and place names are left in their Linear A form.
The language is not yet identified — these are concept-level renderings,
not translations from a known tongue.

---

## The Libation Formula (IOZa2)

The fullest version of the prayer used on every stone offering table:

> O [deity of the peak], at this gathering: be hallowed.
> Of the sanctuary, to the Mother — this libation.
> May it endure.

The minimum form, found on some vessels, is simply the opening invocation:

> O [deity], [we offer to you].
